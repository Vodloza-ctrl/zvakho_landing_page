This is not a passive upload platform.

Artists accepted into ZVAKHO are expected to push their purchase links, announce releases, send fans into WhatsApp checkout, and treat the dashboard as a revenue tool.

Monetization model

Built around direct revenue.

  • Sell singles and albums through artist-specific WhatsApp purchase flows.
  • Use preorders and exclusive release windows before wider distribution.
  • Add merchandise once fulfilment, demand, and supplier reliability are clear.
  • Add VIP subscriptions for artists who can consistently provide exclusive content.
Payout logic

Transparent records first.

ZVAKHO tracks confirmed payments, pending orders, fulfilment status, and artist-level revenue. The goal is to make every sale visible and traceable before payout conversations begin.


For music and VIP access, the operating assumption is artist-first revenue sharing. Merch is handled separately because production, packaging, delivery, and design ownership affect the economics.
