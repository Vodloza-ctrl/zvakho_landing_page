Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you join

How does Zvakho Music work for fans?

Fans opt in on WhatsApp, choose an artist, and unlock drops — tracks, behind-the-scenes clips, early access and exclusive content. You can leave anytime by replying STOP.

Is Zvakho Music a streaming service?

No. Zvakho Music is a direct-to-fan platform. Artists earn through subscriptions and music sales inside WhatsApp. No algorithm dependency. No streaming payout uncertainty.

Can any artist join Zvakho Music?

Zvakho Music is curated. Zvakho Music is a product of Yasibo Media (PBC). We onboard a limited number of serious artists to maintain quality and trust for fans. Partnership enquiries can be sent to support@zvakho.co.zw.
